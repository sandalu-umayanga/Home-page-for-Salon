Welcome to Hair Day

Located in the heart of Madison, Wisconsin, Hair Day is your boutique destination for expert haircuts, vibrant color, and trendy styling. Our warm and inviting atmosphere, combined with a reputation for edgy and modern looks, ensures you leave feeling refreshed and confident. In addition to hair services, we offer makeup and nail treatments, helping you achieve your complete beauty goals. Due to limited availability for coloring appointments, we encourage you to book in advance. Stay inspired between visits with our online tutorials, where our skilled team shares their beauty secrets.
